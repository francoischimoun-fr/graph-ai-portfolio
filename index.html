About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an 8‑week, live online certification that equips professionals with no‑code AI skills to automate data analysis, content creation, and routine workflows.

Key Modules

  • Documents, Presentations & Websites – LLM fundamentals, content generation, no‑code development
  • Automated Data Analysis – Julius, ChatGPT Code Interpreter, Windsurf dashboards
  • GenAI for Research & Text Analysis – Elicit, LiteRev, knowledge synthesis
  • Custom GPTs & Chatbots – RAG systems, Botpress, API integrations
  • AI Workflows & Task Automation – Lindy & Make.com orchestration
  • GenAI for Communication & Outreach – Ideogram, InVideo AI
  • Capstone Project & Showcase – design & peer feedback
